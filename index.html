About Me

Hi! I'm Savannah — a creative, ambitious, and family-oriented digital strategy student at the University of Florida. I'm also a mom to a sweet toddler who keeps life exciting, messy, and full of purpose.

I run a photography business where I capture families, couples, maternity, and lifestyle moments. Photography lets me blend creativity with emotion and serve my community in a meaningful way.
